Core Features

Nine functional modules that deeply address study abroad pain points

01

Consultation & AI Smart Q&A

An intelligent Q&A system built on vast amounts of real international student experiences. We have collected and organized tens of thousands of WeChat group chat records, public account articles, and practical guides from seniors, providing precise study abroad guidance through AI technology.

Coverage Areas

  • Course selection strategies & visa application experience
  • Anti-scam housing guides & essential supplies shopping
  • Bank account opening procedures & insurance selection advice
  • 24/7 intelligent Q&A for instant answers
02

Peace of Mind Plan - Exclusive Safety Assurance for Parents

Professional safety assurance and emergency coordination service, giving parents true peace of mind. When parents cannot contact their child, the platform immediately activates emergency response mechanisms, providing assistance through volunteer networks and professional security forces.

Service Process

  • Parents report lost contact, platform responds immediately
  • Network-wide broadcast notification, volunteer network activated
  • Same-school/same-city users assist in finding contact
  • Professional security/911 services coordinated when necessary
  • Complete progress visualization and tracking feedback

* Platform serves as information coordinator, not a medical/legal/emergency rescue service provider

03

Airport Pickup & New Student Landing Services

Comprehensive pickup services from airport to campus for new students, plus essential setup guidance for enrollment. Through partnerships with local service providers, ensuring new students quickly adapt to local life.

Service Content

  • Free shuttle booking - Regular group pickup between airport and campus
  • Paid private transport - Personalized service through licensed transport partners
  • Setup guidance - Phone plans, bank accounts, SSN and other essential procedures
  • New student groups - Join mutual support communities through offer verification
04

Local Discounts & Merchant Partnerships

Establish exclusive partnerships with restaurants and shops around campus, providing exclusive discounts for students. Through points systems and membership benefits, students gain real value from daily spending.

Partnership Models

  • Exclusive discount coupons - Special pricing at campus-area restaurants
  • Points system - Earn points from purchases to redeem more discounts
  • Merchant verification - QR code verification ensures authentic savings
  • Membership benefits - Different tiers enjoy different perks
05

Second-hand Trading & Job Information

Forum-style information publishing platform supporting second-hand item trading and campus job information sharing. Through chat facilitation features improving transaction efficiency, integrated with professional job platforms for more opportunities.

Platform Features

  • Categorized feeds - Precise categorization by item type and job field
  • Secure chat - Built-in chat features protecting user privacy
  • Credit rating - User credit system based on transaction history
  • Professional partnerships - Collaborate with job platforms for more opportunities
06

Alumni Network - High-Trust Professional Network

Alumni-only high-quality alumni network, ensuring member authenticity through alumni email verification. Providing high-value services including job referrals, homecoming events, and immigration experience sharing.

Value Proposition

  • Strict identity verification - Alumni email verification ensures alumni status
  • High-quality referrals - Genuine job referral opportunities
  • Experience sharing - Immigration, job search, and entrepreneurship experience exchange
  • Homecoming events - Regular alumni gatherings and professional networking
07

Activity Management & Registration CRM System

Complete activity management solutions for student unions and organizers. From activity publishing to on-site verification, to data statistics and analysis, one-stop solution for all aspects of event organization.

Management Functions

  • Dynamic forms - Customize registration forms based on activity type
  • Capacity control - Real-time capacity display and overflow protection
  • QR code verification - On-site scanning to confirm participant identity
  • Data export - One-click export of participant lists in Excel format
08

Volunteer Hour Tracking System

Minute-precise volunteer service duration recording system, providing official data support for various scholarship and honor applications. Supports application requirements for important honors like the President's Volunteer Service Award.

System Features

  • Precise timing - Check-in/check-out accurate to the minute
  • Automatic accumulation - System automatically accumulates total volunteer hours
  • Categorized statistics - Record by activity type and service content
  • Official reports - Generate official format certificates meeting application requirements
09

Home Appliance Services

Comprehensive home appliance solutions for international students, from pre-move-in appliance procurement to daily maintenance and repair, one-stop solution for all home appliance needs in student life.

Service Content

  • Appliance rental - Partner with local merchants for refrigerator, washing machine rental services
  • Purchase guides - Essential appliance buying guides and brand recommendations for new students
  • Repair coordination - 24-hour appliance malfunction reporting and technician dispatch service
  • Second-hand trading - Safe appliance transfer platform among students
  • Group buying discounts - Organize same-school student group purchases for wholesale prices
  • Moving assistance - Graduation season appliance disposal and moving services

Peace of Mind Plan Detailed Rules

Service Scope

  • Emergency response service for lost contact (activated when parents cannot contact students)
  • Volunteer network assistance (covering major US university cities)
  • Professional security coordination (connecting with local security/911 in emergencies)
  • Real-time progress tracking (parents can view processing progress)

Usage Conditions

  • Mutual consent for parent-student information binding
  • Students can optionally authorize location information
  • Service only activated in genuine lost contact situations
  • Cooperation in providing necessary contact information

Service Boundaries

  • Platform serves as information coordinator, not a medical institution
  • Not a legal consulting or legal service provider
  • Not a direct provider of emergency rescue services
  • For emergencies, please contact 911 or relevant local institutions directly

Fee Description
