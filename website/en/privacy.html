Pomelo Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 15, 2025

Last Updated: August 15, 2025

Version: v1.0

1. Policy Overview

Welcome to Pomelo! We are a comprehensive service and safety platform specifically designed for international students and parents studying abroad in the United States. From pre-departure preparation to local life, from activity registration to emergency coordination, we are committed to providing professional services throughout the entire study abroad journey. This privacy policy details how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information. We understand the importance of personal information protection, and commit to strictly comply with relevant laws and regulations including the People's Republic of China's Personal Information Protection Law, Cybersecurity Law, as well as data protection laws in your country and region.

Please read this policy carefully before using our services. If you do not agree with any content of this policy, please do not use our services. Continuing to use our services indicates your agreement to the terms of this privacy policy.

2. Information Collection

2.1 Information You Provide

When using Pomelo services, you may need to provide us with the following information:

  • Registration Information: Legal name, English nickname, affiliated school, school email address, account password
  • Contact Information: Chinese or US phone number (choose one required)
  • Identity Verification: Referral code information, SMS verification code, student offer verification
  • Activity-Related: Activity registration information, dynamic form submissions, personal preference settings
  • Volunteer Service: Volunteer activity participation records, check-in/check-out times, service duration data
  • Peace of Mind Plan: Parent-student binding information, emergency contact information, location authorization (optional)
  • AI Consultation: Question content, school-related inquiries, life service needs
  • Commercial Information: Second-hand trading posts, job information, merchant discount usage records
  • Communication Information: Customer service inquiries, feedback suggestions, partnership communication content

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

To provide better service experience, we may automatically collect the following technical information:

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, app version, device identifiers
  • Usage Data: App usage statistics, feature click records, usage duration
  • Network Information: IP address (used only for technical support and security protection)
  • Push Information: Device push tokens (for sending notifications)
  • Location Information: Collected only with your explicit authorization, used for activity location recommendations

2.3 Special Category Information

We do not actively collect sensitive personal information (such as race, religious beliefs, political views, health status, etc.). If you voluntarily provide such information during activity registration or communication, we will handle it strictly according to legal requirements.

3. Information Use Purposes

We collect your personal information only for the following legitimate purposes:

3.1 Basic Service Provision

  • Create and manage your user account (referral code registration system)
  • Verify your identity and contact information (SMS verification, offer verification)
  • Process activity registration and dynamic form management
  • Provide AI intelligent Q&A and study abroad guide services
  • Peace of Mind Plan parent-student mutual binding service
  • Volunteer check-in/check-out and hour tracking
  • Generate activity QR codes and verification codes
  • Airport pickup service booking and new student life guidance
  • Local merchant discounts and coupon services
  • Second-hand trading and job information posting
  • Alumni network high-trust services

3.2 Service Optimization and Improvement

  • Analyze user behavior to improve product features
  • Personalized recommendations for relevant activities
  • Statistical analysis of user preferences to optimize user experience
  • Technical issue diagnosis and repair

3.3 Communication Services

  • Send account verification emails
  • Push activity registration confirmation notifications
  • Send important service update information
  • Respond to your inquiries and technical support requests

3.4 Security Assurance

  • Prevent fraud and abuse
  • Protect user account security
  • Maintain platform operational order
  • Comply with relevant legal requirements

4. Third-Party Service Integration

To provide complete service experience, we integrate the following trusted third-party services:

4.1 Email Services

  • Gmail SMTP/API: Used to send registration verification emails and activity notifications
  • Data Processing: Email addresses, email content
  • Privacy Protection: Google provides enterprise-level data protection

4.2 Cloud Storage Services

  • Cloudflare R2: Securely store activity images and user avatars
  • Data Processing: Image files, document files
  • Privacy Protection: GDPR compliant, encrypted data storage

4.3 Push Notification Services

  • Firebase FCM: Send important notifications and activity reminders
  • Data Processing: Device push tokens, notification content
  • Privacy Protection: Google Firebase security standards

4.4 Authentication Services

  • Google OAuth: Optional third-party login method
  • Data Processing: Basic user information (only when you authorize)
  • Privacy Protection: Only obtain necessary public information

Important Commitment: We will not share your personal data with unauthorized third parties. All third-party services have undergone strict security review and signed data protection agreements.

5. User Rights

According to relevant laws and regulations, you have the following rights regarding your personal information:

5.1 Right to Know

  • Understand the types of personal information we collect
  • Understand the purposes and methods of personal information use
  • Understand the storage period of personal information
  • Understand personal information sharing situations

5.2 Right of Access

  • View personal information we have collected about you
  • Understand personal information processing situations
  • Obtain copies of personal information

5.3 Right to Rectification

  • Correct inaccurate personal information
  • Supplement incomplete personal information
  • Update outdated personal information

5.4 Right to Deletion

  • Delete personal information that is no longer needed
  • Withdraw consent for personal information processing
  • Request deletion of illegally collected personal information

5.5 Right to Restrict Processing

  • Restrict processing of certain personal information
  • Stop certain data processing activities
  • Require only storage without processing specific data

5.6 Data Portability Rights

  • Obtain personal data in structured, commonly used formats
  • Transfer data to other service providers

6. Minor Protection

We pay special attention to the protection of minors' personal information and strictly comply with relevant legal provisions:

6.1 Age Restrictions

  • Under 14 years old: Must obtain guardian consent to use our services
  • 14-18 years old: Recommended to use our services under guardian guidance
  • Over 18 years old: Can independently use all service functions

6.2 Special Protection Measures

  • Apply stricter security measures for minor information
  • Limit the scope of use of minor information
  • Provide convenient guardian contact channels
  • Regularly review minor data processing situations

6.3 Guardian Rights

Guardians have the right to view, correct, and delete personal information of minors under their guardianship, and can exercise these rights through our customer service channels.

7. Data Security Measures

We adopt industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information:

7.1 Technical Security Measures

  • Data Encryption: Use AES-256 encryption algorithm to protect sensitive data
  • Transmission Security: Full-site HTTPS encrypted transmission
  • Access Control: Strict permission management and authentication
  • Security Monitoring: 24/7 security monitoring and anomaly detection
  • Data Backup: Regular data backup and disaster recovery

7.2 Management Security Measures

  • Employee Training: Regular data protection training
  • Minimum Permissions: Employees can only access data necessary for work
  • Audit Logs: Record all data access and operations
  • Security Assessment: Regular security risk assessments

7.3 Physical Security Measures

  • Servers hosted in security-certified data centers
  • Strict physical access control
  • Environmental monitoring and disaster prevention

8. Data Storage and Cross-Border Transfer

8.1 Data Storage

  • Storage Location: Your personal information is primarily stored on servers within China
  • Storage Period: Retained only for the period necessary to achieve processing purposes
  • Deletion Mechanism: Automatically deleted or anonymized after the retention period expires

8.2 Cross-Border Transfer

In the following situations, your personal information may need to be transferred outside of China:

  • When using third-party services (such as Google services)
  • When providing localized services for overseas users
  • Other circumstances required by laws and regulations

Security Assurance: We ensure that personal information transferred abroad receives adequate protection, complying with data protection legal requirements in both China and destination countries.

9. Cookies and Similar Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to improve user experience:

9.1 Cookie Types

  • Essential Cookies: Ensure basic website functionality operates normally
  • Functional Cookies: Remember your preference settings
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand website usage

9.2 Cookie Management

You can manage cookies through browser settings, but disabling certain cookies may affect website functionality. We do not use cookies to track your activities on other websites.

10. Policy Updates

We may modify this privacy policy due to legal changes, business development, or technical updates. We will notify you of important updates through the following methods:

  • Send notifications within the app
  • Send update explanations via email
  • Post announcements in prominent website locations
  • Require you to reconfirm consent (if there are significant changes)

We recommend you regularly review this policy to understand the latest privacy protection measures.
