Frequently Asked Questions

Account & Registration

How to register a Pomelo account?

Pomelo uses a referral code registration system. You need to:
1. Obtain a valid referral code (from organization administrators or activity publishers)
2. Use the Pomelo app to scan the referral code QR code
3. Fill in registration information: legal name, English nickname, school email, etc.
4. Provide a Chinese or US phone number for verification
5. Complete email verification and phone verification

Why do I need a referral code to register?

The referral code system ensures that only genuine members of overseas Chinese student organizations can use our services, helping maintain community safety and authenticity, while enabling organization administrators to better manage members.

What if I forget my password?

The password reset feature is currently under development. If you forget your password, please email info@americanpromotioncompany.com, and our support team will help you reset your password.

Activities & Registration

How to register for activities?

1. Browse activities of interest in the activity list
2. Click on activity cards to view detailed information
3. Click the "Register" button (login required)
4. Fill out the dynamic registration form
5. Receive registration confirmation and QR code after submission

How to attend activities after successful registration?

After successful registration, you'll receive a unique QR code. At the event venue, organizers will scan your QR code for verification. Please save this QR code, we recommend taking a screenshot and saving it to your phone's photo album.

Can I cancel my registration?

The registration cancellation feature is currently under development. If you need to cancel your registration, please contact the event organizers or email our support team.

Volunteer Services

How to record volunteer service hours?

Volunteer service hours are recorded by organization administrators through a dedicated check-in system:
1. Upon arrival at the volunteer service location, provide your phone number to the administrator
2. Administrator checks you in, recording the start time
3. After volunteer service ends, check out, recording the end time
4. System automatically calculates this session's volunteer hours and adds to your total

Can volunteer hour data be used for Presidential Award applications?

Yes! Our volunteer hour recording system is strictly designed according to official requirements, and the recorded data can serve as supporting material for applications to honors like the President's Volunteer Service Award. You can export official format volunteer service certificates at any time.

Technical Issues

What to do if the app won't load properly?

Please try the following solutions:
1. Check if network connection is normal
2. Restart the application
3. Update to the latest version
4. Restart your phone
If the problem persists, please contact technical support with specific details.

What to do if QR code scanning fails?

1. Ensure camera permissions are enabled
2. Scan in well-lit environments
3. Keep phone steady, aimed at the QR code
4. Clean your phone's camera lens
5. If QR code is blurry, contact the provider for a new one

Which devices and system versions are supported?

Pomelo supports:
• iOS: iOS 13.0 and above
• Android: Android 8.0 (API 26) and above
We recommend using the latest operating system version for the best experience.
